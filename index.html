Project Overview

Leading the Komunitas Muda Katolik (KMK) at Binus University was a profound exercise in team building and resilience. The journey came with its own set of highs and lows from the challenges of aligning diverse schedules, handling logistical hurdles, and maintaining member engagement, to the joys of executing successful events and watching a community grow into a tightly knit family. This experience shaped my approach to empathetic leadership and strategic organization.

x

Milestones & Activities

Pioneering the Community

Took the initiative in the early stages of KMK Binus. Focused on building a welcoming environment, establishing core values, and setting up the foundational structure of the organization.

Cross Campus Synergy

Initiated and managed a major collaboration between KMK Binus and KMK Telkom. Navigated cross institutional communication to successfully unite two different campus cultures.

Event Hosting & Public Speaking

Served as the Master of Ceremonies (MC) for internal and external events. Managed the stage, controlled the crowd's energy, and ensured the smooth flow of the programs.

Skills & Key Takeaways

The core soft skills developed through organizational management.

Leadership & Management

🤝 Conflict Resolution: Learned how to mediate differences in opinions and find middle ground solutions during team planning.

📊 Strategic Planning: Gained the ability to design long-term community goals and break them down into actionable event plans.

Communication & PR

🎙️ Public Speaking: Overcame stage fright and developed the confidence to address large audiences clearly and effectively.

🌐 Networking: Built strong interpersonal skills by bridging connections between different universities and student bodies.
